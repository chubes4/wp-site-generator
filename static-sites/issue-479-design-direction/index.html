Bench note method

Repair in small, reversible steps.

The store language favors stewardship over perfection: test first, work with the grain, pause between passes, and keep the original life of the piece visible.

  1. Read the surface.Match scratch, ring, joint, or dry grain to the cabinet drawer.
  2. Test quietly.Use the hidden-area swab before touching the visible face.
  3. Work patiently.Follow timed passes, not force; stop when the finish settles.
  4. Record the care.Keep the included label card with dates, materials, and future notes.
