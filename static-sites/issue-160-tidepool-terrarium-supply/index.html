Build method

A simple modular system, not a full aquarium commitment.

  1. 1

    Pick a vessel

    Choose an open bowl, lidded cylinder, shallow display tank, or compact glass form based on where it will live.

  2. 2

    Layer the shore

    Add drainage, sand, gravel, stones, shell markers, and driftwood to create a visible tidepool slope.

  3. 3

    Plant for humidity

    Place mosses, ferns, bog plants, and small accents using the included map and trimming tools.

  4. 4

    Mist and refresh

    Use care cards, misting bottles, substrate refills, and seasonal packs to keep the scene clean and intentional.
