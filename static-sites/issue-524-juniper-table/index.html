Small gatherings

Reserve the back table for birthdays, book clubs, and quiet celebrations.

Juniper Table can host small private dinners, daytime meetings, and simple catering trays. Send the date, headcount, accessibility needs, and the kind of meal you have in mind.

Visit

214 Juniper Avenue
near the Maple Street stop

Street parking is easiest on Juniper after 5 p.m. Two short-term spaces are available behind the café for pickup and mobility access.

Access notes

Everyone gets a seat.

The entrance is step-free from Juniper Avenue, the restroom has grab bars, and staff can set low-sensory corner seating when requested.
