Measure twice, order the right tray of parts once.

The shop centers confidence tools: printable fit cards, shade silhouettes, fitter diagrams, and opacity comparisons. Each product page names the compatibility question it answers.

Needs harp and finial; check center drop and shade height.

Uno fitter

Seats at socket; verify ring size and socket style.

Clip fitter

Clips to bulb; best for candle and small accent lamps.
