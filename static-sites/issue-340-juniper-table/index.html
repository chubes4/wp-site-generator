Owner’s note

A room kept at the pace of the block.

Juniper Table grew from a simple habit: making enough soup for whoever might stop by. The café keeps mornings useful for neighbors, lunches easy to understand, and supper nights small enough that the room still sounds like conversation.

Expect ceramic cups that do not match, a chalkboard that changes before the website does, and staff who remember whether you like the window seat or the corner with the outlet.
