First-time friendly

We built the room around not knowing where to start.

Hearthline is for people who like the idea of board games but do not want to decode a wall of boxes alone. Our hosts ask a few simple questions: how many people, how much reading, how competitive, and how long you want to stay.

Then we set you up with a short list, teach the first turn, and check back before the rules get tangled.

House rules

  • No prior game knowledge expected.
  • Ask for a new recommendation anytime.
  • Family tables welcome ages 10+ before 7pm.
  • Quiet corner available for lower-sensory play.
  • Outside games welcome when tables are open.

Questions we hear a lot

A little certainty before you walk in.

Do I need to bring a group?

No. Beginner Night and open-table socials are designed for solo visitors and pairs who want to meet people gently.

What if I do not know any games?

That is normal here. Hosts can recommend, set up, and teach your first round.

Can teens visit?

Yes. Families and teens are welcome, with age-aware recommendations and earlier evening tables.

Is the space accessible?

The main entrance is street-level, aisles are kept wide, and two lower-sensory tables can be requested.
