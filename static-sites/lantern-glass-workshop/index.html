Repair workflow

From old damage to cared-for glass in four practical steps.

  1. 01

    Identify the issue

    Use repair labels and measurement templates to record cracks, loose came, cloudy residue, or missing finish pieces.

  2. 02

    Choose a kit

    Pick by panel type: transom, cabinet, salvaged panel, or small leaded-glass window.

  3. 03

    Prep the bench

    Set out gloves, cleanup cloths, bench mat, pattern paper, foil, patina, polish, or soldering tools as needed.

  4. 04

    Finish and log

    Clean, label, and record the repair in a restoration logbook so the original character stays traceable.
