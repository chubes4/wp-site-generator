The ritual

A complete print day, not a scattered supply list.

  1. CollectPack a pocket press, specimen envelopes, field scissors, and labels.
  2. ComposeArrange botanicals, lace, feathers, or found objects under acrylic.
  3. ExposeUse pre-treated paper or fabric with contact frames and clips.
  4. RinseReveal the blue in trays with rinse bottles, gloves, and drying twine.
  5. ArchiveLog the date, light, subject, and finish in sleeves or a print logbook.
