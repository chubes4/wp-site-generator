The cyanotype ritual

Collect, compose, expose, rinse, archive.

01

Collect

Gather leaves, feathers, lace, pressed flowers, and small found objects in envelopes or a pocket press.

02

Compose

Arrange silhouettes beneath acrylic or glass so stems, shadows, and negative space stay still.

03

Expose

Let sun and time pull the image through treated paper, cotton, cards, or tote blanks.

04

Rinse & archive

Wash, dry, label, sleeve, and log the finished Prussian-blue record of the walk or season.
