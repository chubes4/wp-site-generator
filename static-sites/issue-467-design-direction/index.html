One-sitting method

A repair routine that belongs beside laundry, not in a hobby room.

  1. 01Name the wear. Match the textile to the household problem on the kit label.
  2. 02Prepare the cloth. Wash, dry, trim stray threads, and pin the patch or seam line.
  3. 03Work the visible stitch. Follow the card for durable, plain stitches that look intentional.
  4. 04Return it to use. Note the repair date on the label and keep leftover materials for the refill drawer.

Kept-cloth standard

Repairs are designed to be visible without feeling decorative for decoration’s sake. The point is usefulness with evidence of care: a strengthened corner, a clean seam, a towel that earns another year.
