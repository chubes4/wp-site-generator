Classes & workshops

Make your first bowl, return for a glaze session, or bring the family for a Saturday table.

Every class includes orientation, materials guidance, cleanup expectations, and a plain-language explanation of what happens after your piece goes into the kiln.

Beginner

Wheel 101

Four evenings on centering, pulling walls, trimming, and choosing food-safe glazes.

  • Adults and teens 15+
  • Tools, clay, and two firings included
  • Small cohorts of eight

Open studio & kiln access

For hobby potters who need reliable time, clear shelves, and someone to ask.

Open-studio members reserve wheel or table time, store in-progress work, use shared glazes, and add pieces to scheduled bisque and glaze firings. A studio monitor is present during member hours for safety, kiln questions, and gentle troubleshooting.

Visit

Easy to reach, easy to understand before you arrive.

Hearthline sits on a quiet corner near bus routes, bike racks, and two-hour street parking. The entry, main classroom, glaze sink, and bathroom are on one level.

First-timer notes

Wear comfortable clothes, trim long nails if you can, and expect clay on your hands. Aprons, towels, and cleanup sinks are provided.

Age guidelines

Adult classes are 18+, teen wheel sessions are 15+, and family workshops welcome children 6+ with a participating adult.

Transit & parking

Two bus lines stop within a block. Bike racks are beside the front window. Street parking is easiest after 5 PM.

First-visit FAQs

Do I need experience?

No. Wheel 101, hand-building samplers, and family clay sessions are built for people starting from zero.

When do I get my finished piece?

Most work needs drying, bisque firing, glazing, and glaze firing. Plan on three to five weeks depending on the firing calendar.

Can I bring outside clay?

Members may bring approved clay bodies after orientation. Outside clay must be labeled and suitable for the studio kiln temperature.

Is the studio accessible?

The main spaces are on one level, with movable stools and tables. Contact the studio before class if you want to talk through specific access needs.
