Rehearsal room

A modest room that local bands can actually book.

Afternoon and early evening rehearsal blocks are available when the venue is not hosting a show. The room includes a vocal PA, two microphones, basic stands, four chairs, and a house keyboard by request.

  • $18 per hour for local artists and student groups
  • Two-hour minimum on weekends
  • Load-in from the alley entrance beside Harbor Steps
  • Quiet finish by 9:00pm on rehearsal nights

Location & transit

18 Harbor Steps, near the lower pier gates. Two bus lines stop at Bay & Mercer; bike racks sit beside the public stair rail.

Accessibility

Step-free entry is available from the alley door. The front stair route has eight steps. Ask staff for reserved seating or a lower-volume area.

House rules

No alcohol sales, no re-entry after 8:00pm for minors, respect the posted listening policy, and keep the sidewalk clear for neighbors.

Origin story

From storage room to steady cultural stop.

The room began as a borrowed practice space behind a waterfront repair shop. After a run of winter benefit shows, neighbors helped turn it into a small legal venue focused on safe, early programming for people who wanted music without a late-night bar environment.
