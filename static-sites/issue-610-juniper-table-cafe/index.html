About the owners

Built by people who wanted a place between home and going out.

Owners Mara and Theo opened Juniper Table in the old Bell’s Grocery after years of hosting potluck dinners in their apartment courtyard. The café keeps the counter casual by day and lets the room stretch into something more communal after work.

Expect handwritten menus, practical food, a few mismatched chairs, and staff who can tell you whether the corner table is best for laptop work or a stroller nap.

Visit

Easy to find, easy to enter, easy to ask.

We are on the 6 bus line and three blocks from the Alder light-rail stop. Bike racks sit outside the front windows. Street parking is usually easiest on Juniper after 5 PM and on 7th during weekday mornings.

Accessibility: Step-free front entrance, accessible restroom, flexible seating, high chairs, and a quiet corner available on request. Call ahead if you want us to hold a lower table near the door.
