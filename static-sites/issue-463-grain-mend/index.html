Bench guide

Every kit follows the same calm rhythm.

Repair work feels better when the sequence is visible. The instruction sheets are built to keep the project finite, reversible where possible, and tidy enough for a kitchen table.

01

Read the furniture

Match the problem card to the symptom: wobble, scratch, lift, or drag. Compatibility notes help you pause before the wrong material touches the piece.

02

Dry-fit the fix

Use included gauges, templates, and test strips before glue, wax, or blade work. The kit tells you what “ready” should feel like.

03

Mend in measured passes

Work in small applications with cure times and grit numbers printed in the margin, then stop before a repair becomes a refinish.

04

Return it to use

Final checks cover load, sheen, drawer travel, and cleanup so the piece can go back to daily life with its character intact.
