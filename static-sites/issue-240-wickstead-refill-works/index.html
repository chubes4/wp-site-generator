Ledger guidance

Wick sizing starts with the vessel mouth.

Measure the inside diameter before buying. Then test burn in the room where the candle will live.

Vessel width Common vessel Wax suggestion Wick start Burn-test note
1.75–2.25 in. Votive cup Coconut-soy ECO-2 Trim to 1/4 in.; test 2 hr.
2.5–3 in. Saved glass jar Soy refill CD-8 / CD-10 Watch full melt pool by hour 3.
3–3.5 in. Crock or tin Beeswax blend CD-12 / wood thin Check vessel heat after 90 min.
0.75–1 in. Dinner taper Taper wax 2/0 cotton braid Keep upright; avoid drafts.

Paper caution label

Make the refill useful, labeled, and repeatable.

  • Use heat-safe glass, ceramic, or metal only; skip cracked, thin, or unknown vessels.
  • Record wax type, fragrance load, pour temperature, cure date, and first burn result.
  • Label every gift tin or refill jar with trimming, supervision, and surface warnings.
