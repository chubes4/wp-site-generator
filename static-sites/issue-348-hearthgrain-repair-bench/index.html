Before / after confidence

Know what to do before you open the bottle.

Before

Check the piece, not the trend.

Look for movement, missing parts, finish type, and whether the damage is structural or surface-level. The kit card tells you where to test and what to avoid.

After

Leave evidence of good care.

A repaired piece should feel stable, clean, and honest to its age. We favor blended edges, sound joints, and matching hardware over glossy replacement shine.

Bench note: three measurements solve most orders.

  1. Hole spacing for pulls and hinges
  2. Groove width or cane hole diameter
  3. Scratch depth: finish only, through color, or missing wood
