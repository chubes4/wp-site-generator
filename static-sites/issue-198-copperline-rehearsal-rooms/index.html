LOUD-BUT-SAFE

House policies in plain language

  • Volume: Full-band rooms are built for loud sessions; hallway doors stay closed during play.
  • Minors: All-ages use is welcome. A guardian contact is required for youth groups and student bands.
  • Teachers: Instructors can request recurring blocks and share arrival notes for students.
  • Clean handoff: End five minutes early for cable wrap, trash, and the next group’s setup.
