Measure, choose, finish

A bench-craft path for every shade project.

  1. 01Measure the base

    Record socket height, harp size, top and bottom diameter, and room use.

  2. 02Choose the structure

    Select wire frame, rings, spider fitting, uno adapter, riser, reducer, or clip-on hardware.

  3. 03Pick the covering

    Choose styrene, linen, cotton print, parchment, handmade paper, or silk-look fabric.

  4. 04Finish the edges

    Bind with trim cord, seam tape, glue, clips, and edge rollers for a clean, durable repair.
