Bench craft made approachable

Measure, build, finish, and keep useful light in service.

  1. 01

    Measure the base

    Use shade proportion guides and bulb-spacing cards to choose a diameter, height, and fitting style.

  2. 02

    Choose the frame

    Select wire frames, top and bottom rings, spider fittings, uno adapters, clip-ons, harps, risers, reducers, and finials.

  3. 03

    Wrap the covering

    Cut styrene, linen, cotton print, parchment, paper, or silk-look fabric with templates, cutters, and clips.

  4. 04

    Finish the edge

    Use binding tape, trim cord, seam tape, adhesives, edge rollers, repair labels, and care cards for repeatable results.
