Studio workflow

A small studio still deserves a firing system.

Use shelf maps before loading, batch tags while drying, witness cones during firing, and logbooks after unloading. The catalog is arranged around the repeatable rhythm of home-studio firing instead of broad warehouse categories.

Shop the workflow
  1. PrepWash shelves, mark ware boards, stage posts.
  2. LoadPlace witness cones, map the shelf, tag batches.
  3. FireRecord pyrometer notes and cone behavior.
  4. ReviewFile glaze tests and update the next firing plan.
