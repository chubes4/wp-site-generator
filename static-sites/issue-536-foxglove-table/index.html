The old corner grocery

Renovated, not erased.

Foxglove Table keeps the bones of the former market: tall front windows, painted shelves behind the bar, a pressed-tin ceiling, and the habit of recognizing people when they walk in.

The kitchen cooks in the language of seasonal comfort food—simple plates, careful sourcing, and enough flexibility for tonight’s menu to feel alive.

Private gatherings

A small room for birthdays, visiting families, and team dinners.

Foxglove Table can host seated groups in the back half of the dining room or on the patio in season. Menus stay concise: shared starters, two mains, vegetables for the table, and a dessert passed family-style.
