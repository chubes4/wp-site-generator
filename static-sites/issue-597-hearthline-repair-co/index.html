Find your repair

Tell us what wore through. We’ll tell you what belongs beside it.

Customers often know the tear but not the next move. Hearthline organizes materials by damage pattern so a practical fix can still look intentional.

01

Frayed edge

Use binding stitches, folded patches, and threads that can handle repeated laundering.

02

Thin spot

Layer a compatible fabric behind the wear and build visible reinforcement before a hole opens.

03

Clean tear

Stabilize the opening, mark a stitch path, and make the mend part of the textile’s pattern language.

04

Stained patch zone

Cover what cleaning cannot lift with dyed cloth and a stitch card scaled to the mark.
