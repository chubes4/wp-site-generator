Labeled bench cards

Common repairs we can assess.

Accepted work depends on parts, safety, and time. These categories are a practical starting point, not a promise to repair every item.

Small appliances

Toasters, kettles, fans, mixers

Cords, switches, loose housings, cleaning access, and simple part replacements.

Lighting

Lamps and task lights

Socket checks, plug replacement, shade hardware, cord strain relief, and safer wiring advice.

Soft goods

Backpacks, seams, zippers

Stitching, pulls, straps, patches, snaps, and everyday gear reinforcement.

Furniture tune-ups

Wobbly chairs and small tables

Loose joints, felt feet, tightened hardware, glue-up guidance, and stability checks.

Not a fit for drop-in: microwaves, gas appliances, large furniture, cracked screens, medical devices, or anything that cannot be carried safely by one person.
