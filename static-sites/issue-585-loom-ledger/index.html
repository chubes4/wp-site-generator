The repair ledger

A practical rhythm for household textiles.

  1. Assess

    Choose the household textile, locate wear points, and match the repair to how the piece is used.

  2. Patch

    Layer backing cloth, visible patches, or reinforced edges with thread that complements the room.

  3. Record

    Keep a small care note so future mends become part of the linen’s history, not a flaw to hide.

Repair is part of owning better linens

Start with one softened corner, one honest patch, one more year of use.
