Before you come in

Plain answers for first-timers.

Do I need a partner?

No. Bouldering uses shorter walls and padded floors, so you can climb on your own schedule.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Socks help with rental shoes. We have changing rooms and cubbies.

Will someone explain the grades?

Yes. Route tags show difficulty, start holds, finish holds, and suggested beginner zones.

Can I just watch first?

Absolutely. The front lounge looks onto the easiest wall, and staff can talk through what you are seeing.
