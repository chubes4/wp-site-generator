Bench method

Four steps designed to lower the fear of making it worse.

  1. 01

    Identify the piece

    Choose sofa, chair, trunk, desk good, strap, or handle so the kit uses the right supply quantities and touch points.

  2. 02

    Patch-test in low light

    Use the included card and underside test area to preview conditioner darkening and pigment dry-down before the visible pass.

  3. 03

    Work in thin passes

    Apply balm or color in controlled layers, with ruled wait times and cloth direction printed beside each kit component.

  4. 04

    Refill only what ran out

    Keep the burnisher, gauges, and cards. Order matched balms, thread, straps, or hardware by refill code.
