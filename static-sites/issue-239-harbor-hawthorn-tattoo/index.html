Aftercare guidance

Keep the healing process boring, clean, and watched.

Every appointment leaves with written bandage timing, washing instructions, moisturizer guidance, and a contact path for irritation questions. If something feels off, send a clear photo in natural light.

01

Bandage timing

Follow the artist’s exact removal window. Wash hands before touching the tattoo.

02

Wash gently

Use mild unscented soap, lukewarm water, and a clean towel pat-dry.

03

Moisturize lightly

Thin layers only. Heavy ointment can trap moisture and slow healing.

04

Pause sun and friction

Avoid swimming, direct sun, tight clothing, and intense workouts while it settles.
