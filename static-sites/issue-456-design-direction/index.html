Visit basics

Walk in knowing the room.

Built in a former coin laundry, the space keeps the practical parts visible: clear counters, steady light, clean paths, and posted expectations.

Getting here

Two blocks from the 6 bus. Bike staples by the mint-tile window. No parking lot; side streets are calmer after 6.

Room care

All-ages means watch volume, keep exits clear, ask before filming, and leave a chair for someone who needs one.

Why it works

A noticeboard, not a velvet rope.

Corner Wash House keeps the venue small enough to feel accountable and the cafe practical enough to use on ordinary days. The stage is a shared neighborhood surface: handmade, legible, and warm after dusk.
