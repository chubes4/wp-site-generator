Guided buying

No obscure parts list required.

Every Shadeform kit starts with the lamp you own: base height, fitter type, diameter, room, and fabric weight. The store translates those details into a clear materials list and a kit that arrives ready to assemble.

    Use the included sizing chart to choose diameter, drop, and shade shape.

  2. 02Select the finish

    Pick lining, trim, binding tape, and a fabric pack by room or mood.

  3. 03Build with cards

    Follow project cards that explain adhesive timing, rolling, clipping, and edge finishing.
