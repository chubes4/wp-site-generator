Instruction and planning

Identify the chair before you fill the cart.

Use chair identification guides, seat measurement templates, weave-count cards, and repair logbooks to record the opening, groove, hole count, cord path, material choice, and finish notes.

  • Measure the seat opening and note rounded or trapezoid corners.
  • Match existing cane webbing pattern, rush size, reed width, or cord type.
  • Choose pegs, wedges, awls, clamps, and trays only when the project calls for them.
  • Save the repair logbook page for the next chair in the set.
