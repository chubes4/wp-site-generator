Decision aid

Choose in sequence, not by guesswork.

  1. Surface: flat panel, curved edge, missing corner, or decorative border.
  2. Species: match grain direction first, then color and pore pattern.
  3. Adhesive: reversibility, open time, and clamping pressure determine the method.
  4. Trim: saw, scalpel, scraper, or sanding block based on edge visibility.
  5. Finish: test shellac, oil, wax, or filler on the offcut before the object.
