Kitchen-table lab clarity

A disciplined path from stain map to renewed stack.

  1. 01

    Identify and map

    Use fiber-identification cards and stain-mapping worksheets to decide whether to scour, test, tone, or avoid.

  2. 02

    Scour and mordant

    Prep with detergent, scouring agents, alum, tannin, pH checks, and safe handling notes before any color enters the pot.

  3. 03

    Test small

    Run muslin squares or hidden textile swatches with batch numbers, weight ratios, temperature, and timing recorded.

  4. 04

    Dye, rinse, care

    Finish with fixatives where appropriate, dye-safe detergent, wash-test labels, drying clips, and household care cards.
