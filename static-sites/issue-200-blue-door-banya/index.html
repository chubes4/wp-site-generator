First-visit guidance

No secret rules. Just shared care.

We explain bathhouse etiquette in plain language so you can focus on recovery, not guessing what everyone else already knows.

Bring

  • Swimsuit or comfortable bathhouse clothing
  • Water bottle; refill stations are inside
  • Sandals if you prefer your own
  • A quiet phone setting for shared rooms

We provide

  • One towel and locker use with every session
  • Rinse stations, soap, and changing benches
  • Orientation for room order and heat limits
  • Staff support for cold-plunge timing

Shared-space norms

  • Rinse before sauna, steam, and plunge
  • Sit on a towel in heated rooms
  • Keep conversations neighborly and brief in quiet hours
  • Step out whenever your body asks

Health considerations

Heat and cold are not right for every body or every day. If you are pregnant, managing a heart condition, recovering from illness, or unsure about cold exposure, check with a clinician and tell our desk team what support you need.
