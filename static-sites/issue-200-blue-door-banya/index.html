Neighborhood heat · steam · cold plunge · rest

A calm local place to warm up, cool down, and know what to expect.

Blue Door Banya is a neighborhood sauna and bathhouse for apartment dwellers, runners, cyclists, service workers, older neighbors, and friends looking for an easy non-bar evening. The routine is practical: heat, rinse, cold, rest, hydrate, repeat if it feels right.

First-visit guidance

No secret rules. Just shared care.

Bathhouse etiquette is explained in plain language so you can focus on recovery instead of guessing what everyone else already knows.

Bring

  • Swimsuit or comfortable bathhouse clothing
  • Water bottle for refill stations
  • Sandals if you prefer your own
  • A quiet phone setting for shared rooms

We provide

  • One towel and locker use
  • Rinse stations, soap, and changing benches
  • Room-order orientation
  • Staff support for cold-plunge timing

Shared-space norms

  • Rinse before sauna, steam, and plunge
  • Sit on a towel in heated rooms
  • Keep voices lower during quiet hours
  • Step out whenever your body asks

Health considerations

If you are pregnant, managing a heart condition, recovering from illness, or unsure about cold exposure, check with a clinician and tell our desk team what support you need.
