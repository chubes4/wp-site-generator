Bench notes

Short guidance for patient repairs.

Loose chairs

When to reglue instead of adding screws

Screws can split rails and telegraph through old joinery. A clean reset often keeps the piece truer to how it was built.

Bench note · 6 min

Veneer

Matching grain before matching color

A close grain direction makes a patch quieter even before stain or shellac enters the repair.

Bench note · 4 min

Finish

Testing a ring mark before you polish

Some white marks sit in wax, others in the finish. Start with the gentlest test and stop when the cloud lifts.

First-project path

Not sure what to buy? Start with the visible symptom.

Pick the problem you can describe in one sentence. The kit page lists what is included, what tools you need at home, and where to pause if the piece needs a professional conservator.

  1. It wobbles.Choose a joint reset kit before adding hardware.
  2. A chip shows raw wood.Choose veneer patch materials and color only after test fitting.
  3. A ring looks cloudy.Choose a finish recovery kit and work from mild to stronger steps.
