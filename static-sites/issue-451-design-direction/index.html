Bench method

Quiet steps, strong outcomes.

  1. Read the wear. Identify whether the cloth is thinning, tearing, unraveling, or pulling under weight.
  2. Choose the visibility. Match tone-on-tone restraint, honest contrast, or a deliberate patch block.
  3. Stabilize before beauty. Work from the sound cloth outward so the mend carries stress, not decoration alone.
  4. Finish for use. Press, trim, and test the textile in the way it will actually be handled at home.
