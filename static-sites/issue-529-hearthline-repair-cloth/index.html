Material taste, repair logic

Curated patch bundles by fiber and weight.

Rather than asking you to sort through a wall of notions, Hearthline groups supplies into tactile bundles: featherweight linen for napkins, soft cotton for quilts, sturdy canvas for bags, and drapey blends for curtains.

Naturally dyed threads

Clay, indigo, walnut, madder, oat, and smoke palettes chosen to look intentional on lived-in cloth.

Tools that earn their place

Needles, marking pencils, thimbles, and small rulers selected for hand repairs at a kitchen table.

Practice before the piece

Swatches let first-time menders test stitch density and patch placement before touching an heirloom.
