First-fitting guide

A clear fitting starts before the first pin.

Wear or bring the pieces that affect the garment’s shape. Good preparation helps the tailor mark accurately and explain what can, cannot, or should not be changed.

Bring to your fitting

  • The shoes or heel height you will wear with hems, gowns, suits, or uniforms.
  • Undergarments, layers, shapewear, braces, pads, or performance gear that affect fit.
  • A clean garment whenever possible; stains, odor, or grit can limit safe handling.
  • Event date, pickup needs, and any travel plans so timing is discussed early.
  • Reference notes only when useful — comfort, movement, modesty, or work requirements matter more than trend photos.
