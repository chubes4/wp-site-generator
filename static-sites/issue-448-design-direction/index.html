Questions we answer every week

Plain-language policies for a safer clubhouse.

Can my dog come just once in a while?

Sometimes. We prioritize dogs with predictable attendance because familiar groups stay calmer. Occasional care starts with the same trial process.

Do you take puppies?

Yes, after vaccine milestones are met. Puppy days are shorter, quieter, and focused on confidence rather than long play sessions.

What happens if my dog needs space?

We use rest bays, handling notes, and group changes first. If the day is not working, we call early instead of pushing through.

Will I get photos?

Yes, but not constant staged updates. We share honest snapshots and useful notes when they help you understand your dog’s day.
