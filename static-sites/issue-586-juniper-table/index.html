Behind the counter

Food that feels cared for, never formal.

Juniper Table is run like a local kitchen first: warm coffee in the morning, a concise lunch board by midday, and a room that can become a supper table after work. The menu changes with the market and the calendar changes with the block.

Our aim is simple: make it easy for nearby residents, remote workers, young families, and regulars to know when we are open, what is cooking, and which evenings are worth lingering for.

The room

Old windows, bentwood chairs, food close to the pass.

Private gatherings

A small room for birthdays, team dinners, and cookbook clubs.

Juniper Table can host quiet private gatherings on select evenings when the community calendar is open. The room works best for groups who want warmth over polish: family-style food, a practical beverage list, and a host who knows when to step back.

  • Comfortable for 10–24 guests seated
  • Family-style seasonal menus or coffee-and-cake formats
  • Accessible entrance, nearby bus stops, and limited bike parking
  • Simple inquiry process by email or phone
