Sizing without guesswork

Measure the lamp, then choose the kit.

Shadewright bundles are organized by the dimensions that decide whether a shade feels balanced: bottom diameter, top diameter, vertical height, fitter type, and harp clearance.

  1. 01Measure the base height

    Start from table to socket so the shade lands in proportion.

  2. 02Pick the shade shape

    Drum for clean lines, empire for vintage taper, pleated for soft texture.

  3. 03Match material to light

    Linen diffuses warmly; paper holds crisp pleats; block prints turn the lamp into a room accent.

Repair instead of replace

Common shade failures have small, specific fixes.

Cracked styrene

Rebuild the shade wall while keeping a good frame and favorite outer fabric as a reference.

Loose binding

Use fresh tape, trim, and adhesive to sharpen the edge and hold the lining in place.

Wrong height

Swap harp hardware and finials so the shade sits where the lamp was meant to glow.
