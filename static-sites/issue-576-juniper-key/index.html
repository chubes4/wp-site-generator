The room

Intimate, accessible, and easy to understand before you arrive.

Juniper & Key seats 54, with bar seats facing the upright, a few low tables, and a standing rail by the windows. The main entrance is step-free, the restroom is accessible, and staff can reserve a lower-noise table when you contact us ahead.

Private happy hours

A small room for birthdays, cast parties, and team nights that need a piano.

We host early-evening gatherings before the public music calendar begins, with light bites, cocktail packages, and optional pianist add-ons. The room works best for 18–45 guests.

Musician bookings

We book listeners-first players.

Pianists, vocal accompanists, and compact jazz trios can send a short note with sample recordings, repertoire range, preferred nights, and any accessibility needs. We prioritize musicians who can hold a room without overpowering it.
