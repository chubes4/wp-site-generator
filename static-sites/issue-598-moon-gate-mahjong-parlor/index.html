House etiquette

Keep the room generous.

  • Explain before correcting. New players learn faster when the table stays patient.
  • Keep phones off the felt. Photos are fine between hands; the table stays focused during play.
  • Share the seat. Open play rotates so walk-ins and solo visitors can join.
  • Ask about scoring. House nights use beginner-friendly scoring unless a table agrees otherwise.
