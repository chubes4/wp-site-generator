First visit guidance

Start with a temperament assessment, not a sales call.

We look for comfort with handling, recovery after excitement, dog-to-dog manners, and whether our routines match your dog’s needs.

  1. Send records. Upload current vaccine documentation and basic care notes.
  2. Book a calm visit. Schedule a 45-minute assessment during a low-traffic window.
  3. Build the plan. Choose daycare days, boarding eligibility, or social-hour fit with staff guidance.
