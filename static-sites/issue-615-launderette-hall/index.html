Origin story

The dryers went quiet. The room did not.

When the laundromat closed, the neighbors kept asking what could happen in a bright corner room with tile floors, big windows, and a ceiling low enough to make every chorus feel close. Launderette Hall keeps that everyday usefulness: a place to gather, try something out, and be home before it is too late.
