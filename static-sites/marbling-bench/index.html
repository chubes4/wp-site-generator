A working surface-design bench

From sizing bath to drying line, every shelf has a job.

The storefront follows the real order of a marbling session so buyers can replenish what they used, upgrade a tool, or choose a project bundle without rebuilding the whole supply list.

  1. Mix the bathChoose carrageenan or methylcellulose, then check pH and consistency.
  2. Prepare the sheetUse alum, cotton paper, labels, and edition notes before color touches water.
  3. Float colorDrop pigment, tune dispersant, and test metallic accents on swatch cards.
  4. Comb the patternPull rakes, combs, and styluses through the surface for repeatable designs.
  5. Dry and archiveClip sheets, record formulas, and pair finished papers with bookcloth or stationery blanks.
