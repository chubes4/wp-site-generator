Group safety essentials

Small items stop being afterthoughts.

Before descent

  • Route card reviewed
  • Water level matched to dry storage
  • Rope lengths and pull cord verified

At each rappel

  • Anchor inspected and logged
  • Webbing and rapide link checked
  • Whistles and gloves in reach

After exit

  • Rope washed and dried
  • Sand brushed from devices
  • Repair tape and first-aid modules restocked
